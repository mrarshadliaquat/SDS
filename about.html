Access Control
Access Control Device is a type of security system that uses iris recognition technology to provide access control to a facility or area. It typically consists of a device that captures and analyzes the unique pattern of the iris, which is the colored part of the eye surrounding the pupil, to verify the identity of an individual and grant or deny access accordingly.
